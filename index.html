Killing Romeo. Shakespeare + Medical Ethics.

It's Shakespeare's tragedy, Act 5 Scene 1. You are the poor apothecary of Mantua. An unknown teenager knocks at your door, wanting to buy poison at a high price. What do you do? This serious game brings modern problems of poverty and drug trading into historical relief - try it with students or theater friends, and get ready to dive deep.
