Killing Romeo. Shakespeare + Medical Ethics.

Remember the classic Shakespearean tragedy about teenager lovers in Verona? How about the poor apothecary of Mantua who sells one of them poison? This serious VR potion-making game brings that scene to life for your pre-med and English students alike. Use the game in class to help students re-think the play's themes of poverty, necessity, fate, and addiction.
